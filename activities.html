Spring : Mar - May | 9⁰-20⁰C

When the Sakura bloom (Hanami)

Osaka Castle Park

The park around the castle is filled with cherry trees and they make for some excellent photos with the castle in the background. Some of the trees are illuminated during cherry blossom season.

Learn more

Osaka Mint Bureau

A short walk from Tenmabashi Station on the Keihan Line, the Osaka Mint (Osaka Zoheikyoku) is Osaka’s most popular cherry blossom spot. Opening dates vary from year to year, but they tend to be in early to mid-April.

Learn more

Ryokuchi-koen Park

In Northern Osaka, near Ryokuchikoen Station on the Midusuji subway line, this spacious park has some lovely trees. This park contains the excellent Open-Air Museum of Japanese Farmhouses, which makes a great addition to cherry blossom viewing.

Learn more

Summer: Jun - Sep | 25⁰-36⁰C

The festivals (Matsuri)

Summer Sonic Festival

The Summer Sonic Festival, also known as Supersonic, is an annual two- or three-day rock music festival held at the same time in Osaka and Chiba, Japan.

Dates

19 - 20 Aug, 2023

Learn more

Sumiyoshi Matsuri

The Sumiyoshi Matsuri is a traditional Japanese festival and it happens over three days in and around Osaka's Sumiyoshi Taisha shrine.

Dates

30 Jul - 1 Aug, 2023

Learn more

Aizen Festival

The Aizen Festival is touted as the first major summer festival in Osaka of the year. Along with the Tenjin Festival and the Sumiyoshi Festival, it’s also known as one of the three big Osaka festivals.

Dates

30 Jun - 1 Jul, 2023

Learn more

Autumn: Sep - Nov | 9⁰-21⁰C

The Changing Leaves

Hoshida Park Suspension Bridge

One of the most stunning autumn viewpoints in the whole of Japan. Offering views across the 105 hectare park, the largely wooden suspension bridge is 50 meters high and allows you to look down across the trees.

Entry

free

Learn more

Expo ‘70 Commemorative Park

This large park was once used to host the pavilions of the Japan World Exposition in 1970. Featuring forests, lawns, a Japanese garden, thousands of cherry trees, and the Momiji Waterfall, it’s a great place to view the autumn leaves.

Entry

£1.60 and possible additional charges

Learn more

Ushitakisan Daitoku-ji

An area of mountains well known for autumn foliage, designated as an Important Cultural Asset. Framed with the colors of autumn, the Tahōtō pagoda, bell tower, and Daishidō hall all make for stunning views.

Entry

free

Learn more

Winter : Dec - Feb | 2⁰-12⁰C

Wonders that warm you up

Osaka Aquarium Kaiyūkan Illuminations

Every evening from 5 o’clock the “KAIYUKAN Night Aquarium” shifts away from its daytime form and reveals its otherworldly side. The music inside changes, and the fish swim amidst the deep blue sea under light like moonlight. It is a beautiful and mystical sight.

Dates

around 18 Nov - 5 Mar

Learn more

Universal Studios Japan No Limit! Christmas

Universal Studios Japan is hosting a “Red Hot Christmas” — an energetic party with everyone’s favorite characters: Hello Kitty, the Sesame Street gang, Minions, Snoopy, and more all dressed up in Christmas costumes and dancing along to Christmas songs.

Dates

around 11 Nov - 9 Jan

Learn more

Ten-nen Onsen Naniwa no Yu

A 100% natural hot spring located within 2km from Osaka Station. Naniwa's hot water is kind to everyone, it offers both indoor and outdoor hot springs, saunas and a restaurant.
